TOKYO Rails Meetup

The TOKYO Rails Meetup Group is for web engineers who are using, or are interested in learning more about the Ruby on Rails framework.

We want to encourage all Rubyists and Railers to join us on the first Thursday of every month for good food, drinks and conversation at the COOKPAD offices in Shirokanedai, Tokyo.

The events are free and open to all who register, and we particularly want to encourage both Japanese and English speakers to come and join in.
